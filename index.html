About Me

Hi, I'm Nashrah, an instructional designer and educator with 7+ years of teaching experience across K-12 and higher education settings.

I'm currently pursuing a Master's in Educational Innovation, Technology & Entrepreneurship at UNC Chapel Hill, where I focus on designing impactful learning experiences at the intersection of education, healthcare, and emerging technologies.

My background spans classroom teaching, curriculum development, and digital learning design. I bring a learner-centered approach to every project — whether that's building immersive VR experiences, designing e-learning modules in Articulate Storyline, or mapping stakeholder ecosystems to address real-world challenges like anti-bullying in schools.

Immersive Learning Through VR (Virtual Reality)

Interactive Learning | VR Experience

I created a Virtual Reality (VR) environment using a software called FrameVR that hosted a town center that delivers an English Language learning experience for children ages 3-6 who come from a non-native English speaking family. The objective was to create a learning course that is gamified and makes use of best practice frameworks to effectively teach english-language learners how to adopt english words and phrases commonly used in a familiar setting. Increase learning experience by 40%.

Corporate New Employees Onboarding

Corporare Course Design

This is a short, scenario-based microlearning course built in Articulate Rise 360 to help first-time corporate employees understand everyday workplace expectations. The course focuses on practical skills that are often not formally taught, including: • Interpreting dress codes and workplace norms • Writing clear, action-oriented emails using the pyramid structure • Understanding and using common corporate jargon • Communicating professionally and respecting colleagues’ time.

FPG Internship: From Research to Prototype

AI for STEM Early-Childhood Educators
